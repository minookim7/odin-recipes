Baked Spaghetti

This Spaghetti recipe is from the great chef himself, Maminoo. No other words are needed to describe this beauty.

Ingredients
  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 jar meatless spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 5 tablespoons of butter, melted
  • 2 cups small curd cottage cheese, divided
  • 5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a baking dish.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir beef and onion until meat is browned and onion is soft and translucent, about 7 minutes. Drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and seasoned salt.
  4. Which eggs, Parmesan, and butter in a large bowl.
  5. Place spaghetti mixture in the prepared dish. Top with cottage cheese, mozzarella, and the meat sauce. Cover with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake in oven for 40mins. Remove foil and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella. Continue to bake until the cheese is melted and lightly browned, 20, 25mins longer.
