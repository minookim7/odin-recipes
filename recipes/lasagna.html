Lasagna

This lasagna recipe is from the great chef himself, Maminoo. No other words are needed to describe this beauty.

Ingredients
  • 1 (26 ounce) jar Prego Traditional Italian Sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Quarter cup of grated Parmesan cheese
Steps
  1. Spread pasta sauce onto a baking dish. Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup of pasta sauce. Top with remaining noodles and remaining pasta sauce. Cover.
  2. Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour, then uncover and top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Let it stand for 5 minutes.
